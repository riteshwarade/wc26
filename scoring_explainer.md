# How scoring works

In Group stage, it was 2 points for every correct result you called. Simple.

For Knockout stage, picks are worth more the deeper into the tournament you go:

| Round | Points |
|---|---|
| Round of 32 | 4 |
| Round of 16 | 8 |
| Quarterfinals | 12 |
| Semifinals | 16 |
| 3rd place | 12 |
| Final | 24 |

One thing to know: if a team you picked for a later round gets knocked out before they get there, that pick is worthless, you can't score those points. So picking teams that make deep runs really matters.

Best of luck!
